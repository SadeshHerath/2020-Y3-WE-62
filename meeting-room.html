MEETING & FUNCTION ROOM

It is the latest and trendiest contemporary Ballroom in the city; a venue with amenities that'll make any event sparkle. This multipurpose hall is a perfect place for Glamorous Weddings, Sociable Dinners, Engagement Parties, Awards Ceremonies, Product Launches, High Tech Conferences, to name a few. With plenty of space options, you can host up to 800 guests for a seated dinner in the main hall with smaller rooms available for smaller party sizes. Hidden behind its modern looks, the Grand Ballroom boasts of a Unique Lighting System, Large Screens, Public Address system and Data Projector.

  • Maximum: 150 pax
